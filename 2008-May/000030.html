Hello Mauricio,

This is something similar to call that I used in Ubuntu's
gnome-voice-control package. Remove the current installation with
"make uninstall", then call configure like this:

./configure --prefix=/usr --libexecdir=/usr/lib/gnome-voice-control
--mandir=\$${prefix}/share/man --infodir=/usr/share/info
--with-sphinx2=/usr

Then do the usual "make", "make install". Hope it works :).
