I agree with nsh.  Just make your own collection of prompts.  Make
sure you use stuff that no one will claim the copyright to.

For example

use sayings (I am sure you can find lists of Spanish sayings on the
Internet, and most of them will be quite old, and practically no one
will know who used it for the first time).

Copy and paste single sentences from weather forecasts.  They contain
very simple sentences like: tomorrow will be a sunny day... the
maximum temperature for tomorrow will be twenty degrees.

Or use old (out of copyright) books, however avoid using tongue
twisters (sentences that are really hard to pronounce) and words that
no one uses anymore.

I'm sure you can think of many more examples like those

Robin
